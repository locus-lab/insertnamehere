Clearing the Skies

A game name that's poetic, for once

How To Play

Objective:

  • Survive in the barren wasteland that Earth has become.
  • Develop your colony and manage its resources.
  • Explore the wastes and see what's still out there...
Navigate the map using right click and drag
Select structures from the menu, then click on parts of the map to place them.
